Ormar

A challenging clone of the old, popular arcade game Snake.

Controls

Left arrow
Turns the snake left
Right arrow
Turns the snake right

To restart, refresh the page.

The snake can warp between the edges of the screen.

Food types

Green food (basic)
+1 length, +10% energy
Violet food (normal)
+3 length, +100% energy
Violet food (evil)
+3 length, -40% energy
Blue food (nom nom nom)
+3 length, +30% energy
Red food (it buuuurns!)
+5 length, -20% energy

Powerups

Colored squares are powerup abilities. Powerups can have both beneficial and harmful effects to the snake.

Energy

The meter at the bottom of the play area is your energy meter. When your energy decrease, your snake will be blacked out for longer periods of time. When the snake runs out of energy, it dies.
